About Me

Get to know me

A brief bio

Ever since I was a kid, I loved being creative. Usually, children play with action figures, make arts and crafts, play games, etc. As a child, my friends and I would create little movies, podcasts, and websites about random things going on in our lives. When I was younger, I thought of it as just a hobby, but I soon realized my passion for technology could become a career. At Penn State, I am currently pursuing a bachelor's degree in Information Sciences & Technology with a minor in Security Risk Analysis to further my knowledge. Additionally, I am on the Division I Fencing Team, which has taught me to be responsible, driven, and goal-oriented.

Outside of academics and athletics, I love to travel. I have been lucky enough to travel worldwide and visit several diverse places. In the summer of 2019, I interned in Rome, Italy. During my time overseas, I enjoyed working at a local company where I learned about different cultures and understood other working styles. In the future, I hope I will have the opportunity to travel more, whether that's for fun or a future job.
